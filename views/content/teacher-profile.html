Caron Williamson is a 200 hour Yoga Alliance certified yoga teacher. She trained in traditional Hatha yoga in India. Caron enjoyed a successful international corporate career before becoming a mother and moving to India from London in 2011. She began a journey of self-discovery through yoga and is deeply committed to understanding and living the ancient wisdom of yoga which emphasises the relationship between body, breath, mind, emotions and soul. She is also a freelance illustrator and writer.

Caron brings a personal approach to her classes in order to bring peace and joy to the lives of her students.

Caron hopes to help her students understand that yoga is much more than simply asana practice,and that through our body and breath we can begin to access deeper parts of ourselves that will lead to personal transformation and increased joy
